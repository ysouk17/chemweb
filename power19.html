THE ROLE OF POWER–19 IN SUSTAINED FARMING

POWER–19 (N.P.K. 19:19:19) is a WATER SOLUBLE FERTLIZER CONCENTRATE.

POWER–19 is a fertilizer that contains both macro and micro nutrition materials inevitable for plant together with vitamins. It is a product recommended for balanced and healthy fertilization of plants. It is recommended to be used during growth period of plants. It plays a considerable role in increasing the yield without disturbing the soil balance as well as easily mixes into the soil and plants

POWER–19 is one of the internationally known formulations as a complete fertilizer.

Power - 19 fertilizer covers the entire range of plant nutrients required for excellent plant nutrition when efficient fertilizers and fertilization methods have become the only answer to the ever-growing demand for enhancing farm produce. Modern agriculture must supply crops with optimal rates of nutrients throughout the growth cycle in the most efficient manner possible, and without degrading soil and water resources. Fertigation enables highly efficient use of nutrients. It is virtually free of detrimental elements and efficiently absorbed by plants. IMPORTANCE OF POWER - 19

  • Power-19 is the fertilizer that contains all primary, secondary and micro nutrition materials and vitamins together in one pack
  • Power-19 is inevitable for healthy growth of plants and sustained high yield of crops
  • Power-19 is a product intended for balanced and healthy fertilization of plants
  • Power-19 is recommended to be used during growth period of plants to ensure healthy vegetative growth
  • Power-19 is a 100% water soluble fertilizer which can be sprayed on all types of crops like rubber, paddy, banana, vegetables, fruit crops, potato, wheat, cotton, pulses etc. for healthy growth and sustained high yield of quality produce
  • Power-19 is most ideal in precision farming
  • Power-19 is recommended to spray at vegetative, flowering and fruit development stages
  • Power-19 is compatible with almost all the common plants
  • Power-19 is an environment-friendly fertilizer, avoids the extravagant use of fertilizers, encourages the balanced and conscious use of fertilizers and increases the use efficiency and yield of crops ensuring maximum yield during the harvest
  • Power-19 is a completely water-soluble fertilizer containing a range of rich balanced nutrients for soil and soil-less greenhouse crops and suitable for dissolution in water
  • Power-19 is made of high quality nutrients sourced from fine ingredients of high nutritive value
  • Power-19 is the ultimate answer when efficient fertilizers and fertilization methods have become the only solution to the ever-growing demand for enhancing farm produce
  • Power – 19 is suitable for both foliar spraying and drip irrigation
  • Power -19 is very unique in enhancing nutrient use efficiency
  • Power – 19 is absorbed rapidly and effectively by plants
  • Power – 19 is ideal for short duration crops.

 

DIRECTIONS FOR USE

Dissolve 5 Gram / ltr. of water and spray on the crop foliage at 2-4 sprays at 15 days interval. Apply 180-200 ltr spray solution per acre. For better use efficiency, spraying is recommended during cool hours of the day.
